Map of East Asia

East Asia has a rich history and often in many cases influenced each other. Dragons, foxes, snakes and ghosts feature prominently in many stories and were often spread to each country, resulting in similar but still unique creatures like the 9 tailed fox, which is known as kumiho or 구미호 in Korean, Huli jing or 狐狸精 in Chinese and kitsune or 狐, キツネ in Japanese. There also features many Buddhist and traditional folk religion influence in each nation that are very familiar as well.
